Welcome to Shri Rupesh Pawar Foundation

Our mission is to help farmers overcome the challenges they face in their daily lives and promote sustainable agriculture practices.

The Farmer Support Foundation is a non-profit Foundation dedicated to providing assistance and support to farmers in need. We believe that by empowering farmers, we can help to create more resilient communities and a more sustainable future for all.

The main aim of an foundation is to provide support and resources to farmers to help them overcome the challenges they face. This support can take many forms, such as financial assistance, training and education, market access, advocacy, and disaster relief. By providing these resources, agricultural foundations help to ensure that farmers have the tools and knowledge they need to thrive in today's rapidly changing agricultural landscape.

We offer a range of services to help farmers improve their productivity and profitability, including financial assistance, training and education programs, market access, advocacy, and disaster relief.

We rely on the support of generous donors like you to continue our important work. You can make a donation to the Farmer Support Foundation today and help us make a difference in the lives of farmers across the country.

If you're passionate about supporting farmers and promoting sustainable agriculture practices, we invite you to join us in our mission. Learn more about our programs and services and become a part of our community today. Together, we can make a difference in the lives of farmers and create a more sustainable future for all.
