About Rupesh Pawar Organization

Overview

Vasudhaiva Kutumbakam the most sublime Vedantic thought of Maha Upanishad 6.71, means, the world is one family .We all are children's of Mother Earth. Therefore, it is our responsibility as family members to cultivate unconditional generosity towards others and respect its natural resources to make it prosper .Vasundhara is Social engineering Institution which is a symbol of symbiotic relationship between environment and humanism for equitable social and economic development . The organization insists on environmentally friendly sustainable economic development and adoption of eco-friendly appropriate technology for caring natural resources and accordingly educate them.

Our Vision :

Vasundhara Vidyaprasarak Vaidik , Krushi Sanskruti Vistarak Sanstha’ is founded & promoted by like-minded catalytic, dedicated & enthusiastic group of experts & professionals with a multidisciplinary social development background with grass - root experiences & global appreciation.
